# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and professional.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project.
- Respect legal and ethical boundaries for cybersecurity work.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Encouraging illegal activity.
- Sharing credentials, private data, or exploit instructions unrelated to defensive research.
- Repeated disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code of conduct.
